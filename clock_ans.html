Clock Question Answers

Answer 1: 8°. Explanation: The second hand completes a full revolution (360°) in 60 seconds. In 480°, the second hand moves for 480°/360° * 60 seconds = 80 seconds. In the same time, the minute hand moves 8° (6° per minute). Thus, the minute hand moves 8°.

Answer 2: 180°. Explanation: The hour hand moves 30° per hour (360° / 12 hours). From 8 o'clock to 2 o'clock, the hour hand moves through 6 hours, thus 6 * 30° = 180°.

Answer 3: 220°. Explanation: The angle between the hour and minute hands at 7:10 can be calculated using the formula for angle between hour and minute hands:
Angle = |(30*hour - (11/2)*minutes)|. At 7:10, the angle is |(30*7) - (11/2)*10| = 210°. The reflex angle = 360° - 210° = 220°.

Answer 4: 22. Explanation: The hands of the clock coincide 22 times in 24 hours, once every hour and 5 minutes, except at 12 o'clock.

Answer 5: 22. Explanation: The hour and minute hands are at right angles 22 times in 12 hours. This happens approximately once every hour and 5.5 minutes.

Answer 6: 22. Explanation: The clock hands are in opposite directions (i.e., 180° apart) 22 times in 24 hours.

Answer 7: 192 1/2°. Explanation: At 10:25, the angle between the hands is 167 1/2°, so the reflex angle is 360° - 167 1/2° = 192 1/2°.

Answer 8: 125°. Explanation: The minute hand is at the 8th position (240°). The hour hand moves 1/2° per minute, and at 3:40, it is 20° from 3 o'clock. So the angle is 240° - 20° = 125°.

Answer 9: 5 2/11 min. past 7. Explanation: At 7:00, the hands are at 210° apart, and they move toward each other. To find the exact time, you use the formula: 11/2 * time = 210 + 180. Solving this gives the answer.

Answer 10: 43 5/11 min. past 5. Explanation: The hands form a right angle twice in an hour. Between 5:30 and 6:00, one of these occurrences is at 43 5/11 minutes past 5.
