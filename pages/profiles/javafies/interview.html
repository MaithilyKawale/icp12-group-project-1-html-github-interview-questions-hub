Java Developer Interview Experience

Company: Infosys

Role: Java Developer

Experience Level: Fresher

Education: B.Tech in Computer Science

Location: Virtual (Online Interview)

Interview Rounds:

Round 1: Online Assessment (60 mins)

  • 15 MCQs on Java, OOPs, Data Structures, and SQL.
  • 2 Coding Questions – Arrays and Strings.
  • Platform: Infosys Web Proctored System.

Round 2: Technical Interview

  • Introduction and explanation of projects.
  • Questions on OOP concepts (Inheritance, Polymorphism).
  • Java-related questions: Exception handling, Collections, Threads.
  • Simple SQL queries were also asked.
  • Duration: 30 minutes.

Round 3: HR Interview

  • Self-introduction.
  • Why Java? Why Infosys?
  • Questions on relocation, shift timing, strengths, and weaknesses.
  • Very friendly and casual conversation.
  • Duration: 10 minutes.

Tips for Preparation:

  • Revise Java basics thoroughly – OOPs, Collections, and Exception Handling.
  • Practice coding questions on arrays, strings, and recursion.
  • Be confident during project explanation.
  • Stay calm and polite in HR round.

Note: Every experience can differ based on the panel and company updates.
