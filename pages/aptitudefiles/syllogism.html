Welcome to Syllogism Interview Quiz

Q1. Statements: All cats are animals. Some animals are dogs.
Conclusions: I. Some cats are dogs. II. All animals are cats.





Q2. Statements: Some pens are pencils. All pencils are erasers.
Conclusions: I. Some pens are erasers. II. All erasers are pencils.





Q3. Statements: No apple is banana. All bananas are mangoes.
Conclusions: I. No apple is mango. II. Some mangoes are not apples.





Q4. Statements: All windows are doors. No door is wall.
Conclusions: I. No wall is a window. II. Some doors are not windows.





Q5. Statements: Some bikes are scooters. Some scooters are cycles.
Conclusions: I. Some bikes are cycles. II. All cycles are scooters.
