Answers & Explanations - Blood Relation Quiz

Q1. Answer: His son
Explanation: "That man's father is my father's son" — the man has no siblings, so “my father’s son” is himself. That means “that man’s father is me,” so the man in the photo is his **son**.

Q2. Answer: Brother
Explanation: "Only son of my grandfather" = Veena's father. So, the boy is the son of Veena's father = her **brother**.

Q3. Answer: Niece
Explanation: “My father’s only daughter” = Monika herself. So Reena is the daughter of Monika → Reena is Monika’s **niece**.

Q4. Answer: Mother
Explanation: “His mother is the only daughter of my mother” = the woman herself. So the woman is the **mother** of the man.

Q5. Answer: Grandson
Explanation: Y’s son → his son → X is brother of that person = both are Y's grandsons. So X is Y's **grandson**.

Q6. Answer: Cousin
Explanation: “Her mother’s son” = her brother. Nikhil is the only son of Rita’s brother → Nikhil is Rita’s **nephew**, so Rita is his **aunt** → she is his **cousin’s mother** = **Cousin**.

Q7. Answer: Mother’s sister
Explanation: "Son of her only brother = her nephew" is the "brother of my wife" = wife's brother = **brother-in-law**. So the lady is **wife's aunt** = man's **mother’s sister**.

Q8. Answer: Sister-in-law
Explanation: Ketan is Namrata’s **son’s son** (father’s only son = her brother or herself). So Ketan is her **son**, and his mother is her **sister-in-law** (if Namrata is paternal aunt). Correct option: **Can’t be determined**.

Q9. Answer: Son
Explanation: “Wife of my husband” = herself → her daughter’s brother = her **son**.

Q10. Answer: Cousin
Explanation: Brother of mother = maternal uncle → his son = woman’s **cousin**.
