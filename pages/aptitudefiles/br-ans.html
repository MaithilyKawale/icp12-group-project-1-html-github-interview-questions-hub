Answers & Explanations – Blood Relation Page

Ans1. Answer: Cousin
Explanation: The boy is the son of the daughter of the father of her uncle. Her uncle’s father is her grandfather. The daughter of her grandfather is her aunt or mother, and her son is her cousin.

Ans2. Answer: Mother
Explanation: The grandmother of Rahul’s father’s only son is Rahul’s grandmother. Her daughter-in-law is Rahul’s mother.

Ans3. Answer: Nephew
Explanation: The person is the son of Ram’s father’s only daughter (Ram’s sister), so the person is Ram’s nephew.

Ans4. Answer: Daughter
Explanation: The girl is the daughter of the boy’s mother’s only son (the boy himself), so she is his daughter.

Ans5. Answer: Brother-in-law
Explanation: The man’s only brother is the father of Seema’s son, so the man is Seema’s husband’s brother (brother-in-law).

Ans6. Answer: Sister-in-law
Explanation: The woman’s husband’s sister is the man’s sister, so the woman is the man’s sister-in-law.

Ans7. Answer: Husband
Explanation: The woman is the mother of Nitin’s wife’s only daughter, so Nitin is her husband.

Ans8. Answer: Nephew
Explanation: The boy is the son of the daughter of the girl’s mother’s sister (aunt), so the boy is her nephew.

Ans9. Answer: Daughter-in-law
Explanation: The girl is wife of the grandson of Raj’s mother (Raj’s son), so she is Raj’s daughter-in-law.

Ans10. Answer: Husband
Explanation: The man is the husband of the woman’s only daughter’s mother-in-law (the woman herself), so he is her husband.
