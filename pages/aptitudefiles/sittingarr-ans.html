Answers & Explanations – Sitting Arrangement

Ans1. Answer: B
Explanation: B is to the left of C but to the right of A, so the order is A, B, C. Including D and E, the arrangement can be A, B, C, D, E. Hence, C is in the middle.

Ans2. Answer: S
Explanation: T is to the right of Q, so the arrangement could be ... Q - T ... P is to the left of Q. R is between S and T. Arranging these hints in a circular way, S ends up opposite to P.

Ans3. Answer: A
Explanation: A is between C and B, so the arrangement is C, A, B. In a row of 7, the center is the 4th position. Placing this group accordingly, A lands in the center.

Ans4. Answer: S
Explanation: T < S < R and Q is at the extreme right. The possible order is T, S, R, ?, Q. The middle position (3rd) is S.

Ans5. Answer: P
Explanation: If P is between S and T in a circle, then the person to the immediate right of T is P.

Ans6. Answer: A
Explanation: D is at one end, E at the other. C is between A and B, so one possible order is D, A, C, B, E. Hence, A is second from the left.

Ans7. Answer: East
Explanation: Q faces north. Second to the right of Q is East. So, P is sitting on Q’s east, and faces the same direction → East.

Ans8. Answer: 4
Explanation: In a circle of 8, there are 4 pairs sitting exactly opposite each other. (8 ÷ 2 = 4 pairs).

Ans9. Answer: R
Explanation: Facing south: Left and right are reversed. So R is between T and S. Hence, R is in the middle.

Ans10. Answer: Cannot be determined
Explanation: A is opposite D, B is between A and C, but F’s position isn't clearly linked with others. So, the person to F’s right cannot be determined.
