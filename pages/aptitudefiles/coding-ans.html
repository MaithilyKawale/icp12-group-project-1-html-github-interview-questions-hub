Ans8. Answer: 30
Explanation: BALL = 62, CAT = 24 → likely sum of squares of alphabet positions.
D=4²=16, O=15²=225, G=7²=49 → 16+225+49 = 290 (not matching).
But if D+O+G → 4+15+7 = 26 → matching Option A: 26
So correct answer is: 26
