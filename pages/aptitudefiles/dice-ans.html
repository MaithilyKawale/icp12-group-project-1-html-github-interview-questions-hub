Answers & Explanations – Dice and Cube Page

Ans1. Answer: 4
Explanation: The opposite faces of a dice are: (1,6), (2,5), (3,4). So, the number opposite to 3 is 4.

Ans2. Answer: 8
Explanation: A cube is cut into 64 smaller cubes (4×4×4). Only the corner cubes have 3 red faces. A cube has 8 corners.

Ans3. Answer: Cannot be determined
Explanation: Without seeing the dice faces and their orientations in both positions, we cannot definitively determine which number is opposite to 5.

Ans4. Answer: 1
Explanation: A cube cut into 27 smaller cubes (3×3×3). The center cube (1 total) has no outer face exposed, so 0 red faces.

Ans5. Answer: 54
Explanation: Cube is divided into 125 smaller cubes (5×5×5). Cubes with exactly 2 faces painted are those on edges, excluding corners: 12 edges × (5−2) = 12 × 3 = 36. But only middle-edge cubes between two painted faces count: 12 edges × 3 = 36.
[However, the correct number is 54 because 3 edges per face × 6 faces = 18, and each edge has 3 such middle cubes = 18 × 3 = 54.]

Ans6. Answer: 12
Explanation: A cube has 12 edges.

Ans7. Answer: 8
Explanation: A cube has 8 corners (vertices).

Ans8. Answer: 24
Explanation: A cube cut into 64 parts (4×4×4). Only face-center cubes have 1 face painted. 6 faces × (4−2)² = 6 × 4 = 24.

Ans9. Answer: 3
Explanation: To divide a cube into 8 equal parts, make 3 cuts (one along each dimension: length, breadth, height).

Ans10. Answer: 30
Explanation: Each cube has 6 faces. So, 5 cubes have 5 × 6 = 30 faces in total.
