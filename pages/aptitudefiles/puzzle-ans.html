Answers & Explanations – Puzzle Page

Ans1. Answer: West
Explanation: Start facing North → 90° clockwise → East → 180° counter-clockwise → West → 90° clockwise → North.
Final direction: West

Ans2. Answer: 24
Explanation: Total students = (Left position) + (Right position) − 1 = 10 + 15 − 1 = 24.

Ans3. Answer: 12
Explanation: 2 pencils = 1 pen → 1 pencil = 0.5 pen
3 pens = 1 notebook → 1 pen = 1/3 notebook
So, 1 pencil = 0.5 × (1/3) = 1/6 notebook
Therefore, 1 notebook = 6 pencils → 2 notebooks = 12 pencils.

Ans4. Answer: C
Explanation: A > B, A < C ⇒ C> A > B
D < B ⇒ C> A > B > D. So, the tallest is C.

Ans5. Answer: Q
Explanation: Order based on clues:
R < P < Q < S < T
So arrangement is: R, P, Q, S, T → The person in the middle is Q.
