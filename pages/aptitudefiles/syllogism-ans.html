Answers & Explanations – Syllogism

Ans1. Answer: Only Conclusion I follows
Explanation: Statements: All cats are animals. All animals are mammals.
Conclusion I: All cats are mammals. ✅
Conclusion II: Some mammals are cats. ✅ (But this is not a definite conclusion in traditional syllogism rules.)
Only I follows as it is logically deduced.

Ans2. Answer: Neither Conclusion I nor II follows
Explanation: Statements: Some pens are pencils. No pencil is eraser.
Conclusion I: Some pens are erasers. ❌
Conclusion II: All pens are erasers. ❌
Neither can be concluded from the statements given.

Ans3. Answer: Only Conclusion II follows
Explanation: Statements: All dogs are pets. Some pets are tigers.
Conclusion I: Some dogs are tigers. ❌
Conclusion II: Some tigers are pets. ✅

Ans4. Answer: Both conclusions I and II follow
Explanation: Statements: All books are papers. Some papers are pens.
Conclusion I: Some pens are papers. ✅
Conclusion II: Some books are pens. ❌ (not directly implied)
Correction: Actually, only Conclusion I follows.

Ans5. Answer: Only Conclusion II follows
Explanation: Statements: No boy is girl. All girls are kids.
Conclusion I: Some kids are not boys. ❌ (cannot be concluded)
Conclusion II: No girl is a boy. ✅

Ans6. Answer: Both conclusions I and II follow
Explanation: Statements: Some mobiles are laptops. All laptops are tablets.
Conclusion I: Some mobiles are tablets. ✅
Conclusion II: All laptops are mobiles. ❌
Correction: Only Conclusion I follows.

Ans7. Answer: Only Conclusion I follows
Explanation: Statements: All birds are animals. Some animals are insects.

Conclusion II: All birds are insects. ❌

Ans8. Answer: Neither I nor II follows
Explanation: Statements: Some cups are glasses. No glass is jug.
Conclusion I: Some jugs are cups. ❌
Conclusion II: No cup is jug. ❌

Ans9. Answer: Only Conclusion II follows
Explanation: Statements: All flowers are plants. Some plants are trees.
Conclusion I: Some trees are flowers. ❌
Conclusion II: Some plants are trees. ✅

Ans10. Answer: Cannot be determined
Explanation: Statements: Some fruits are vegetables. All vegetables are grains.
Conclusion I: All fruits are grains. ❌
Conclusion II: Some grains are vegetables. ✅ (but this is given, not concluded)
Still, cannot determine any new definite conclusion beyond what's stated.
