Welcome to the Direction Questions Page

Q1. Seven friends A, B, C, D, E, F, and G are standing in a park. B is 5 m east of A. C is 8 m north of B. D is 3 m west of C. E is 10 m south of D. F is 6 m west of E. G is 4 m north of F.

Q2. A man starts walking from point P towards east for 10 m, then turns north and walks 7 m. He turns west and walks 5 m, then turns south and walks 7 m. How far is he from the starting point?

Q3. Points X, Y, and Z lie on a straight line. Y is 9 m east of X. Z is 5 m west of Y. What is the distance between X and Z?

Q4. Six people — M, N, O, P, Q, and R — are sitting around a circular table. M is opposite to P. N is to the immediate left of M. Q is to the immediate right of P. R is opposite to N.

Q5. A person walks 12 m north, then 5 m east, then 12 m south, and finally 5 m east. How far is he from the starting point?

Q6. Two points A and B are 20 m apart on a straight road. Point C is 15 m north of A and point D is 15 m north of B. Find the distance between points C and D.

Q7. A boy walks 6 m south, then turns right and walks 8 m, turns right again and walks 6 m, then turns left and walks 4 m. What is his final distance from the starting point?

Q8. Points P, Q, R, S, and T are on a road. Q is 10 m east of P. R is 6 m west of Q. S is 4 m east of R. T is 2 m west of P. What is the distance between S and T?

Q9. A person starts from point X and walks 8 m north, turns left and walks 6 m, then turns left and walks 8 m. How far is he from point X?

Q10. Five people are standing in a row facing north. A is to the right of B. C is to the left of B but right of D. E is standing at the extreme left. Who is standing in the middle?
