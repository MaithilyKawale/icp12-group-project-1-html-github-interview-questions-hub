Common HR Questions
  1. Tell me about yourself.
    2. – Start with your education, key strengths, and recent activities (like courses or projects).

  2. Why should we hire you?
    3. – Talk about your skills, attitude, and how you can add value.

  3. Why do you want to join our company?
    4. – Show that you've researched the company and align with its goals.

  4. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
    5. – Mention 2–3 strengths and 1 weakness with how you're improving it.

  5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
    6. – Show career growth and commitment to the IT field.

  6. Are you willing to relocate? / Work in shifts?
    7. – Be honest and flexible if you're okay with it.

  7. Tell me about a time you faced a challenge and how you handled it.
    8. – Use a real situation (maybe from college or team projects).

  8. Do you prefer working alone or in a team?
    9. – Say you’re comfortable with both but highlight teamwork skills.

  9. How do you handle pressure or tight deadlines?
    10. – Mention planning, prioritization, and staying calm.

  10. Any questions for us?
    11. – Always ask something like: "What are the growth opportunities here?" or "Can you describe the training process?"
